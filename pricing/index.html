Section 02

Monthly Design Support

Design help when you need it, without hiring in-house.

Bentoji Monthly Design Support is for businesses that need regular creative help, but not enough to hire a full-time designer. It gives you a flexible way to keep your website, marketing, product, brand and visual materials moving from month to month.

Use it for day-to-day design tasks such as website updates, social graphics, campaign assets, presentation design, email banners, PDF layouts, small UX/UI improvements and brand support.

Send requests as they come up. We'll work through them one at a time, based on your priority and monthly plan capacity.

What counts as a small design task?

A small task usually has a clear brief, a clear output, existing content or direction, limited exploration, and a realistic scope for the monthly plan.

Most support tasks are between 30 minutes and 4 hours. Larger requests can still be handled, but they may use more of your monthly capacity or be split into smaller stages.

Fits well

"Can you design three LinkedIn graphics for this campaign?"

Better as a project

"Can you create a full brand identity for our new business?"

What is not included by default

Monthly Design Support is not a replacement for a full project. Larger work can be quoted separately or planned as a project.

  • Full website design or redesign
  • Full brand identity systems
  • Complex UX research
  • Full app or product design from scratch
  • Large pitch decks from scratch
  • Custom development
  • Advanced animation or video editing
  • Print management
  • Hosting, domains, paid fonts or stock assets
  • Urgent same-day work unless agreed in advance

How it works

01

Choose a monthly plan

Pick the level of support that suits your usual design needs.

02

Send design requests

Send requests by email, Notion, Trello or a simple form. A good request includes what you need, the goal, any content or brand files, and the deadline if there is one.

03

One active task at a time

We complete one active request at a time, based on your priority and available monthly capacity. If you have multiple requests, we'll help order them by priority.

04

Review and refine

Each request includes reasonable revisions within your monthly plan capacity.

05

Keep going each month

Unused time rolls over for one month while your plan stays active. Cancel before the next billing cycle if you no longer need ongoing support.
